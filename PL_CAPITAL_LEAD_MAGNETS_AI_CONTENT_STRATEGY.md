## ðŸ“Š Lead Magnet Strategy Overview

### What is a Lead Magnet?

**Definition:** A valuable free resource offered in exchange for contact information (email, phone, LinkedIn).

**Purpose:**
- Capture leads who aren't ready to buy yet
- Demonstrate expertise and build trust
- Segment audience by interest/pain point
- Nurture leads until they're sales-ready

**Effective Lead Magnet Criteria:**
1. **Specific:** Solves one clear problem
2. **Instant Value:** Provides quick win in <30 minutes
3. **Actionable:** Includes steps, templates, tools
4. **High-Perceived Value:** Looks premium, professional
5. **Relevant:** Aligns with target segment's pain points
