### Pain Points & Challenges

   - Legacy systems (manual processes, no automation)

   - Over-reliance on equity (80-90% of revenue)
   - MF trail commissions slashed (SEBI regulations)
   - Insurance difficult to scale (high compliance, long sales cycles)
