**üéØ Product Recommendation Logic by Tier:**

**Tier 1 (Hot - Ultra HNI/HNI Segments):**
- **Primary Strategy**: PMS-focused with tactical Equity and Bond allocations
- **Equity**: 10-20% tactical allocation, F&O for hedging large portfolios
- **MF + Bonds**: 10-20% in corporate bonds and gilt funds for stability
- **PMS**: 60-70% core allocation (AQUA for business owners, MADP for salaried/retirees)
- **AIF**: 50-60% eligible (‚Çπ1 Cr+ threshold met) - hedge funds, real estate, PE strategies
- **Target Profile**: ‚Çπ2-15 Cr portfolios, 45-60 age, high net worth individuals

**Tier 2 (Warm - Upper Affluent to HNI Segments):**
- **Primary Strategy**: Balanced PMS with moderate Equity and MF+Bonds exposure
- **Equity**: 20-30% allocation for growth, selective F&O usage
- **MF + Bonds**: 20-30% in balanced MF portfolio + corporate bonds
- **PMS**: 50-60% core allocation (mix of AQUA and MADP based on risk profile)
- **AIF**: 30-40% eligible (borderline ‚Çπ1 Cr threshold) - accessible AIF structures
- **Target Profile**: ‚Çπ1-6 Cr portfolios, 38-56 age, emerging HNI segment

**Tier 3 (Cold - Mass Affluent to Upper Affluent Segments):**
- **Primary Strategy**: Equity + MF focused with long-term PMS graduation pathway
- **Equity**: **60-80% primary allocation** - Cash equity for wealth building, limited F&O
- **MF + Bonds**: **20-40% primary allocation** - MF SIPs for systematic investing, debt MFs for stability
- **PMS**: 70% MADP (conservative) / 30% AQUA (aggressive) - for ‚Çπ50L+ borderline clients
- **AIF**: 10-20% eligible (majority below ‚Çπ1 Cr threshold)
- **Target Profile**: ‚Çπ20L-2 Cr portfolios, 28-50 age, future HNI prospects, **mass market opportunity**

**üí° Strategic Product Evolution by Tier:**
1. **Tier 3 ‚Üí Tier 2**: Equity + MF wealth accumulation ‚Üí PMS transition at ‚Çπ55L+ milestone
2. **Tier 2 ‚Üí Tier 1**: PMS core allocation ‚Üí AIF addition at ‚Çπ1 Cr+ threshold
3. **Revenue Model**: Tier 3 drives transaction revenue (Equity brokerage + MF trails), Tier 1-2 drive recurring revenue (PMS + AIF management fees)

**üîë Key Product Pairing Insights:**
- **Equity + PMS**: Tactical equity (10-30%) alongside PMS core (60-70%) for HNI/UHNI diversification
- **MF + Bonds**: Conservative allocation (20-50%) for retirees, salaried executives, and women investors
- **Equity + MF**: Primary wealth-building products (80-100% combined) for mass affluent and young professionals
- **PMS + AIF**: Premium combo (70-90% combined) for UHNI clients seeking institutional-grade strategies
