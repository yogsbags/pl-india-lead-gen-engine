## üíº PMS & AIF PRODUCT SUITABILITY ANALYSIS

### PL Capital Product Portfolio

**PMS Products:**
1. **AQUA (Active Quality Undervalued Assets)**
   - Focus: High-quality undervalued stocks with strong fundamentals
   - Strategy: Bottom-up stock picking, value investing approach
   - Target: Wealth preservation + steady growth (15-20% CAGR target)
   - Risk: Moderate to high
   - Ideal For: Conservative HNI, Upper Affluent seeking long-term wealth creation

2. **MADP (Multi-Asset Dynamic Portfolio)**
   - Focus: Multi-asset allocation across equity, debt, gold, REITs
   - Strategy: Dynamic asset allocation based on market cycles
   - Target: Risk-adjusted returns with downside protection
   - Risk: Moderate
   - Ideal For: Balanced investors, pre-retirement HNI, family offices

**Common PMS Features:**
- **Minimum Investment**: ‚Çπ50 lakh (SEBI regulation)
- **Target Segments**: Upper Affluent, HNI, Ultra HNI
- **Management Fees**: 2-2.5% annually + performance-linked incentives
- **Reporting**: Quarterly performance reports, monthly statements
- **Tax Efficiency**: Direct equity holdings for LTCG benefits

**AIF (Alternative Investment Funds):**
- **Minimum Investment**: ‚Çπ1 crore (SEBI regulation)
