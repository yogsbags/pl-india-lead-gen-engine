## What Are Lead Signals?

**Lead signals** (also called **buying signals** or **intent signals**) are behavioral indicators that someone at your target account is actively thinking about a problem your product/service solves.

### Examples:
- Company raises Series B funding â†’ More budget available
- CFO posts on LinkedIn about "wealth management strategies" â†’ Active research
- Company posts 3 new job openings for "Financial Analyst" â†’ Growing team
- Executive changes jobs â†’ New priorities, fresh relationships
- Company visits your pricing page 5 times â†’ High intent
- Company downloads your whitepaper â†’ Problem awareness

## Apollo.io Intent Signals

### Overview

Apollo provides **buying intent data** powered by LeadSift (a Foundry company), tracking over **1,600 intent topics** with **98% accuracy**, refreshed **weekly**.

### Signal Sources

1. **Public Web Sources:** News sites, blogs, forums
2. **Social Networks:** LinkedIn, Twitter activity
3. **Content Engagements:** Whitepapers, webinars, ebooks
4. **Company Websites:** Pricing page visits, demo requests
5. **Events:** Webinar registrations, conference attendance
6. **Hiring Postings:** Job openings indicating needs
7. **Technology Installs:** New software adoptions

### Intent Scoring (Apollo)

Apollo scores intent signals as:

- **High Intent:** â‰¥4 signals in last 90 days OR upward trend with â‰¥2 signals in last week
- **Medium Intent:** â‰¥2 signals in last 90 days AND â‰¥1 signal in last 2 weeks
- **Low Intent:** â‰¥1 signal in last 90 days

### How to Use Apollo Intent

**Step 1: Select Intent Topics**

For **Financial Advisors (IFAs)**:
- "Wealth Management"
- "Portfolio Management"
- "Investment Advisory"
- "Financial Planning Software"
- "Client Relationship Management"
- "Retirement Planning"

For **HNIs/UHNIs**:
- "Private Wealth Management"
- "Family Office Services"
- "Estate Planning"
- "Tax Optimization"
- "Alternative Investments"

**Step 2: Set Up Alerts**

Apollo sends **email alerts** when accounts show intent spikes for your selected topics.

**Step 3: Automate Sequence Enrollment**

Configure Apollo to automatically add high-intent contacts to email sequences.

**Step 4: Personalize Outreach**

Use intent signals in your messaging:
> "Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] has been researching [Intent Topic]. We help [similar companies] achieve [outcome]..."
