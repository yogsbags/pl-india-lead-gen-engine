# Lead Generation Master Strategy

**Created**: 2025-10-03
**Owner**: Marketing & Business Development
**Objective**: Generate qualified leads across 4 target segments with hyperpersonalized outreach

---

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive lead generation strategy targeting 4 distinct segments:
1. **Partners** (IFAs, Wealth Managers, Distributors)
2. **HNIs** (High Net Worth Individuals - ₹5-25 Cr)
3. **UHNIs** (Ultra High Net Worth Individuals - ₹25+ Cr)
4. **Mass Affluent** (₹50 Lakh - ₹5 Cr investable assets)

---

## 8. Compliance & Legal Considerations

### 8.1 Data Privacy
- Comply with Indian data protection laws
- Explicit consent for email marketing
- Clear opt-out mechanisms
- Data retention policies (delete after 2 years if no engagement)
- Secure storage of personal data

### 8.2 SEBI Regulations
- No misleading performance claims
- All performance data must include disclaimers
- "Past performance doesn't guarantee future returns"
- PMS risk disclosures mandatory
- Minimum investment thresholds clearly stated

### 8.3 LinkedIn ToS Compliance
- Avoid aggressive automation (stay within limits)
- Use connection requests judiciously (<100/week)
- No fake profiles or misleading information
- Respect user privacy settings

### 8.4 Email Marketing Laws
- CAN-SPAM compliance
- Clear sender identification
- Physical address in footer
- One-click unsubscribe
- Honor opt-outs within 10 days

## 11. Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Email Deliverability
**Mitigation**:
- Warm up email domain gradually
- Use reputable email service
- Verify emails before sending
- Monitor bounce rates closely
- Use double opt-in for newsletters

### Risk 2: LinkedIn Account Restrictions
**Mitigation**:
- Stay within automation limits
- Use multiple accounts for scale
- Focus on quality over quantity
- Mix automated with manual activity
- Have backup LinkedIn profiles

### Risk 3: Low Conversion Rates
**Mitigation**:
- Extensive A/B testing
- Refine ICP based on early data
- Improve value proposition messaging
- Increase personalization
- Add more social proof

### Risk 4: Data Quality Issues
**Mitigation**:
- Use multiple data sources
- Manual verification for UHNIs
- Regular data hygiene
- Feedback loop from sales team
- Continuous enrichment

### Risk 5: Compliance Violations
**Mitigation**:
- Legal review of all content
- Clear disclaimers
- Opt-out mechanisms
- Regular compliance audits
- Training for team members
